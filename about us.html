ABOUT US

There may have been so many instances where wish you had a car so you could commute to various places in a day so you didn’t have to pay so much for taxis, but at the same time, you don’t want to commit yourself to all the responsibilities of owning a car. This is when renting cars is the best option, because you can commute to various places at a lower rate, and you’ll never have to face the full responsibility of owning cars.

Our Aim

The core idea behind the project is to propose a system that allows users to rent cars at their convenience. This system is fed with various models of cars and their details (like model number, no. of seats, engine type, etc) and the price for renting the car for a day. This system allows users to look for a car from a roster of cars and to rent it to the user for as many days as they wish to. The user will be provided with the invoice at the end of the transaction.
